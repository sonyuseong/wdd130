Overview

Purpose

This website is dedicated to selling tools needed for rafting. While there are many stores specializing in rafting equipment, there are no any that sell them online. Therefore, I aim to create a consumer-friendly website offering a wide range of tools for enjoying rafting.

Audience

The primary customers of this website are men in their 20s to 40s. They are usually people who go rafting with friends or families looking to enjoy light rafting activities. These customers prefer to purchase rafting equipment online. In addition to rafting gear, they also want items that enhance the overall rafting experience. My website will primarily be accessed via laptops or desktops.
