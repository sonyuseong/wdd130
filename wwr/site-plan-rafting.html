Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to introduce Korean comics. Korea has many interesting comics, and a significant number of them are being translated into English. Japanese comics are very popular, and there are numerous websites and resources introduced to them. However, Korean comics are still in the early stages of gaining recognition, so there are not many websites or resources that provide information about them. For this reason, I decided to create a website to introduce Korean comics and related information.

Audience

The target customer is teenagers and young adults, both male and female. They are looking for interesting comics and are recently interested in K-pop, which has sparked their curiosity about Korean comics as well. They have enough allowance to pay for and enjoy comics. While they want to know about Korean comics, they struggle to find a well-organized website that satisfies their needs. They want to know about the genres, popularity, content, and prices of Korean comics. My audience will primarily access my website through mobile devices or laptops.
