Content

Home page

People will come to my site. Because they are curious about Korean webtoons. Typically, those who enjoy comics always want to find interesting ones to read. This is because the speed of updates for comics can’t keep up with the speed that people read them. In this situation, Korean webtoons are great content for those who love comics, and people will visit my site to find information. Visitors will want to search for Korean webtoons that match their interests, based on genre and descriptions. The reason why visitors choose my site rather than others is my site is the only site that introduces Korean webtoons.

Images for the Home page

Searching page

I plan to make this page a detailed search page. It will help visitors search for webtoons with more specific options. The page will provide an overview of various webtoon types and include buttons that assist visitors in finding webtoons of their desired genres.

Images for the Page 2

detail page

I plan to make this page to contain detailed information about the webtoon selected by a visitor. When visitors select a webtoon they are interested in and click on its image, they will be directed to this page. Them, they can see more images of the webtoon, learn about its story, and find out which platforms offer it and at what price. Additionally, this page will introduce other webtoons in genres that visitors might like.
